What is WisBuch?

WisBuch is an online platform aligns with the concept of open access and the idea, that information and education should be freely available to everyone. Where you can read everything while enjoying the processof learning.

Nowadays, libraries can be accessed through digital. These libraries have revolutionized the traditional concept of libraries by enabling users to access information from anywhere in the world. Digital libraries have been around for a while and have evolved over the years. They offer the same services as traditional libraries, but with the added benefit of networking systems.

Digital libraries are especially useful for people who are far away from local or national libraries. They provide access to digital resources such as e-books, journals, and research papers that can be accessed anytime and are flexible (Khan, 2021).




WisBuch (wis-booh)


The german term "Wisbuch" means "Book of Knowledge," with its components derived from the words "Wissen," which means "Knowledge," and "Buch," signifying the word "Book."

These words originated in Germany, where Johannes Gutenberg, the first inventor of movable books, came from. Johannes Gutenberg revolutionized the production of books by introducing the mechanical movable-type printing press that played a crucial role in developing books.
